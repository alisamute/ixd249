Big Bean House

Big Bean House is a local coffee shop with three locations across the East Bay. Known for its exceptional coffee and delicious food, Big Bean House has become a favorite spot for coffee lovers in the area, Their menu features a variety of items, from creamy avocado toast and savory paninis to wholesome oats and refreshing acai bowls.

In addition to their delicious food, Big Bean House also offers their own brand of high quality coffee beans, perfect for brewing at home. Having personally visited a few times, I enjoyed their amazing menu since each dish is made with care and quality ingredients.
