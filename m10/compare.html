Benefits I would bring and what I would change on the website...

  1. Firstly, the website does not show off their aesthetic well. They need to better Convey their unique personality and aesthetic to shoppers, bringing the in-store experience, online.
  2. Improving their aesthetics can make a significant difference. A visually appealing and well-designed website attracts more visitors, and when potential customers see an inviting website for a café, they’re a lot more likely to visit in person.
  3. Each page on the website feels disconnected, almost like they’re from entirely different websites. For instance, looking above comparing pages on the original site shows a lack of consistency. I would fix this by creating a more cohesive design, making sure every page feels like part of the same unified website.
