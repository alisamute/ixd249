Home

The RecordMan inventory numbers over one million pieces.  The Record Man complex consists of three interacting stores—  The Record Man, Planet Mix, and The RecordMan Annex.

The RecordMan offers a virtual wonderland of exciting musical exploration for the avid collector in all genres of music and entertainment.

The awesome collection of LPs, 45s, and 78s is practically unmatched by any store in the country.

Whatever your musical tastes, you will not be disappointed. All types of music are represented—Jazz, Blues, Rock, Country, Classical, Soul, and all other categories. Of course, the selection of used CDs and tapes is extensive and constantly growing.

RecordMan offers DJs and collectors a vast selection of tens of thousands of 45s and12-inch singles. Planet Mix offers a wide array of used video games and systems.

For the eclectic or deep collector, The RecordMan houses books on music and musicians, song books and thousands of pieces of sheet music. The RecordMan Annex inventory of posters, magazines, photographs, ephemera, and music videos is growing rapidly. Imagine a 12-foot high wall packed with vintage prerecorded reel to reel tapes!
